Maintenance Tips

Keep your vehicle running smoothly with these essential maintenance tips from our expert technicians.

Regular Oil Changes

Check your engine oil level monthly and change it according to your vehicle's maintenance schedule. Fresh oil ensures proper engine lubrication and longevity.

  • Check oil levels at least once a month
  • Change oil every 3,000-5,000 miles for conventional oil
  • Change oil every 7,500-10,000 miles for synthetic oil

Tire Maintenance

Check tire pressure weekly and rotate tires every 5,000-7,000 miles. Proper inflation improves fuel economy and extends tire life.

  • Inspect tire tread depth regularly
  • Check for uneven wear patterns
  • Maintain proper tire pressure for better mileage
